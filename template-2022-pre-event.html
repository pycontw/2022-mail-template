Program

Main Track

On the conference days, speakers will have a Q & A session during their office hours on Gather.Town. Refer to PyCon APAC 2022 Schedule for further details.

On Gather.Town, there are four main rooms for keynotes, talks, and tutorials. R0 and R1 are in Space A, while R2 and R3 are in Space B.

After logging into Gather.Town, you can move to the projection booth to watch talks or tutorials.

Open Space

Open Space is located at the bottom center corner of Space B, and runs its main session at 15:45 ~ 16:35 on 4 September (Day 2), but you can also register anytime during the conference days as a minor session..

Unlike the conventional program, Open Space allows participants to design and layout their plans. It allows any attendee to organize a special gathering space. The topic can be anything, even non-technical!

To sign up for your topic of interest, fill out the following form Open Space Registration and you will be granted your own Gather.Town room.

Lightning Talks

Lightning Talks are located at the bottom left corner of Space A, the event times start from 17:25 on Day 1, and 17:55 on Day 2..

The talks will also be featured on YouTube in the R0 broadcast.

Lightning Talks are as fast as lightning. Each speaker has five minutes to present and the clock starts ticking as the slides are presented. The speaker will have to finish their talk in a fast and precise manner. Failing to do so will result in being kicked out of the meeting room.

If you are interested in signing up for one of our Lightning shows, fill out our sign-up sheet Lightning Talk Registration to enter a draw before 13:00 for each day. Registration will roll over to the next day, so the earlier you sign up, the higher the chance of being drawn!
